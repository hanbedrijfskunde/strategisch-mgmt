Exploit

(High Opportunity, Low Risk)

Business Innovation Example:

Implement an AI-powered chatbot for customer service to answer common queries, reducing support tickets and improving response time. This is a proven, low-risk way to increase efficiency.

Explore

(High Opportunity, High Risk)

Business Innovation Example (Moonshot):

Develop a proprietary generative AI model trained on internal data to create novel product designs autonomously. This is a high-risk, high-reward "moonshot" that could create a significant competitive advantage.

Low Opportunity

Learn

(Low Opportunity, Low Risk)

Business Innovation Example:

Run internal workshops and provide access to AI tools (like content generators) for the marketing team to experiment with creating ad copy and social media posts. This fosters learning without impacting core operations.

Monitor

(Low Opportunity, High Risk)

Business Innovation Example:

Assign a research team to track the development of Artificial General Intelligence (AGI). While too risky and nascent for current investment, monitoring progress ensures the company isn't blindsided by a paradigm shift.
